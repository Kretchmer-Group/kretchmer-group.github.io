title: Real-Time Dynamics of Ultrafast Electronic Decay Mechanisms
description: The ionization of an inner valence or core electron can initiate competing electronic relaxation pathways that occur on the attosecond to femtosecond timescale. These include interatomic coulomb decay, in which an electron relaxes to fill the hole while simultaneously ionizing another electron on a neighboring molecule, and electron transfer mediated decay, in which an electron transfers from a neighboring molecule to fill the hole simultaneously ionizing another electron, among others. We develop and apply real-time DFT methods to directly simulate the real-time dynamics of these competing electronic decay mechanisms in molecular clusters and at surfaces.
